At OQ Base Industriesللصناعات الأساسية, we are dedicated to delivering high-quality, essential-base chemicals that power industries and improve lives around the world. Our commitment toالتزامنا من أجل sustainable development, innovation and operational excellence is reflected in everything we do, from producing methanol that supports clean energy solutions to ammonia that bolsters global food security, and the LPG products powering homes and industries across the world.

What We Doأعمالنا

Our Productsمنتجاتنا

As a regional leader in base chemical production, OQBI produces 1.8 million tons per annum (Mtpa) of methanol, ammonia and LPG products, meeting global demand.بصفتنا رواد إقليميون في إنتاج المواد الكيميائية الأساسية، تنتج أوكيو للصناعات الأساسية 1.8 مليون طن سنويًا من الميثانول والأمونيا ومنتجات الغاز البترولي المُسال لتلبية الطلب العالمي.

Production Plantsمحطات الإنتاج

State-of-the-art production in Salalah Free Zoneإنتاج متطور في المنطقة الحرة بصلالة

Our three advanced plants transform Oman's natural gas into essential products for cooking, heating, transport, manufacturing and multiple other uses. Serving over 15 countries across three continents, we drive economies and empower communities.

OQBI's three production plants are located in the Salalah Free Zone (SFZ), which provides corporate tax exemption, and receive feedstock under long-term contracts with the Integrated Gas Company (IGC) through a pipeline network operated by OQ Gas Networks.

Exportsالصادرات

100% of methanol and ammonia production and 87% of LPG products production are exported outside of Oman via OQ Trading (OQ Group's global trading business).
